Komatsu UGSR Battery Hauler Operator Training Simulator

In response to the need for **remote training in India during the pandemic**, I initiated the development of a VR training simulator as a rapid prototype. Using a gaming joystick, pedals, and a VR headset, I created a proof of concept to demonstrate how operators could learn to drive the Komatsu Battery-powered Hauler in an underground mining environment.

After securing executive buy-in, I expanded the prototype into an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) to gauge customer interest. Once demand was confirmed, I worked with:

  • A third-party development team to create a hardware interface allowing real machine controls to be used in my software.
  • Internal mechanical engineers to design a mobile simulator frame integrating the real seat and interface devices.
  • Marketing and branding teams to establish a recognizable product identity.

Key Features

  • XR Immersion: Uses a VR headset and custom-built simulator frame for a realistic training experience.
  • Real Machine Controls: Interfaces with actual Battery Hauler controls for hands-on learning.
  • Physics & Environmental Simulation: Accurately models the Battery Hauler's physics and underground mine conditions.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitors load/unload times, wall collisions, and operator efficiency.
  • 4-Wheel Hydraulic Assist Training: Provides guided practice for hydraulic assist operation.
  • Machine Start-Up Procedure: Simulates real machine logic, ensuring trainees follow correct procedures.
  • User-Centric UI/UX: Designed for intuitive operation and accurate reinforcement of machine handling techniques.
