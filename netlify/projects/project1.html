The **Komatsu UGSR Battery Hauler Operator Training Simulator** is a cutting-edge virtual reality simulation designed to train operators in handling underground battery haulers. This simulator replicates real-world mining conditions with high-fidelity physics, interactive controls, and an immersive training environment.

  • Realistic Physics: Simulates actual vehicle handling, terrain, and load weight effects.
  • Immersive VR Support: Fully compatible with VR headsets for enhanced training realism.
  • Customizable Training Scenarios: Includes different difficulty levels and hazard simulations.
  • Performance Analytics: Tracks operator efficiency, reaction times, and task completion scores.
